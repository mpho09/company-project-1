## Why did you choose this company specifically?
I chose this company because I saw an opportunity to improve its online presence through a more modern, user-friendly design. The existing website had room for improvement in areas such as navigation, visual appeal, responsiveness and overall user experience. I wanted to create a design that better reflects the company's brand while making it easier for customers to find information and engage with its services.

## Why does their website need a redesign?
The visual design looked outdated and did not effectively reflect a modern, professional brand. Navigation was not intuitive, making it harder for users to quickly find important information. The website also lacked a clear visual hierarchy and strong calls-to-action, which could reduce user engagement and conversions.There were accessibility improvements that could be made, such as better color contrast, semantic HTML and keyboard navigation support.
